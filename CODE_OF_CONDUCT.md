# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant. By participating, you agree to abide by its terms. A short summary is below; for the full text, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.

Our commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all, regardless of background, identity, or experience.

Expected behavior

- Be respectful, empathetic, and constructive.
- Raise issues in a civil manner and provide actionable feedback.

Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or demeaning comments.
- Threats, insults, or other abusive conduct.

Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the repository maintainers. Reported incidents will be handled promptly and with care.
